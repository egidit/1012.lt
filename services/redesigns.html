The problem

A facelift won't fix
a broken foundation.

✕

Performance disaster

PageSpeed score under 50. Unoptimized images, render-blocking scripts, no caching strategy. Your site loads like it's on dialup. Visitors don't wait.

✕

SEO graveyard

Broken links, missing alt tags, duplicate titles, no structured data. Google can't figure out what your site is about — so it ranks you nowhere.

✕

Mobile afterthought

60%+ of your traffic comes from phones. Your site pinches, zooms, and breaks on every screen that isn't a 27" monitor. That's not responsive — that's negligent.

✕

Security vulnerabilities

Outdated WordPress plugins, no SSL, exposed admin panels. Your site isn't just slow — it's a liability. One exploit away from defacement or data leak.

The rebuild

Same brand. New everything
under the hood.

01

Performance audit & benchmark

We measure everything first. Core Web Vitals, PageSpeed, TTFB, CLS, LCP. Then we set specific targets and hold ourselves to them. No vague "it'll be faster."

02

Modern tech stack

Off WordPress. Off the bloat. Custom code, CDN hosting, automatic backups, HTTPS by default. Your new site loads in under a second. Every time.

03

SEO migration

301 redirects for every old URL. Updated sitemap, structured data, proper meta tags, cleaned heading hierarchy. You keep your existing rankings and build from there.

04

Mobile-first design

Not "responsive" as an afterthought. Designed for the phone first, then scaled up. Touch targets, readable text, fast interactions on every device.

05

Conversion focus

Redesign isn't just about looking better. We restructure content flow, add clear CTAs, and track what visitors actually do. The before/after difference is measurable.

06

Security hardening

CSP headers, SRI on external scripts, no exposed admin panels, rate limiting, input sanitization. Your new site is built like it has enemies — because it does.
